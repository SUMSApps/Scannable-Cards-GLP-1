Lixisenatide

Lyxumia

Dosage size/strength

10mcg and 20mcg (fixed-dose injection pens)

How it's taken

Once daily injection

 Min-max daily dose

10mcg once daily for 14 days, then 20mcg once daily maintenance dose from day 15 onwards

When taken

Once daily, within the hour prior to any meal of the day. It is preferable that the prandial injection of Lixisenatide is performed before the same meal every day

Times per day

Once
